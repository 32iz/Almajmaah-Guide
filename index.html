About Almajmaah

Al-Majmaah is a Saudi city and is the Emirate of Al-Majmaah Governorate in the Riyadh region in the center of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

It is the capital of Sudair Province in Najd and its largest city. Its construction dates back to 833 AH corresponding to 1412 AD.

It is the center of Sudair District and its largest city. The population of Majma'ah is about 133,285, between citizens and residents.

The number of residential neighborhoods in the city is about 12, and the number of housing units in these neighborhoods is about 4,497 housing units.

Al-Majmaah is the administrative and commercial center of Sudair District.

All central government departments in the province are located in Al-Majmaah, and it has branches in some of its cities and villages.
